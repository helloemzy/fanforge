# FanForge Product Marketing Context

## Non-Negotiables

- ICP: fandom readers and fanfiction writers.
- Voice: fan-first, plain language, never overly technical in marketing copy.
- Positioning: "Public titles, protected chapters."
- Promise boundary: never claim "impossible to scrape" or "cannot be scraped."
- Approved promise: strong anti-scrape defenses + verified access + enforcement.

## Product Truth

- Public pages are indexable for discovery by title and metadata.
- Full chapter text is behind authenticated, verified-email, human-verified access.
- Known AI crawlers are blocked by policy and middleware.
- Reader views include invisible provenance watermarking.
- Works are never licensed by FanForge for model training.

## Forbidden Marketing Claims

- "AI cannot scrape this website."
- "Zero scraping is possible."
- "Guaranteed impossible to copy."

## Allowed Marketing Claims

- "We actively block known AI crawlers."
- "Full chapters require verified member sessions."
- "We use human checks and provenance markers to deter abuse."
- "We do not license fanworks for AI training."

## Launch Priority Order

1. Security and trust controls.
2. Reader/writer UX quality.
3. Growth loops and SEO.

## Current Growth Constraint

- Public discovery is title-level; do not expose full chapter text for indexing.
